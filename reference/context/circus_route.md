## Circus Geography Notes

### Winter Quarters
- **Peru, Indiana** — Known as "Circus Capital of the World"
- **Sarasota, Florida** — Ringling Bros. headquarters
- **Baraboo, Wisconsin** — Ringling Bros. original home

### Key Route Patterns (1950s)
1. **Spring opening**: Florida or Southern states
2. **Summer swing**: Move north as weather warms
3. **Fall return**: Work back south
4. **Winter quarters**: November–February
