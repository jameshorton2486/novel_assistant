# POV_BLEED_RULES.md

This document defines **chapter-specific POV bleed detection rules**.
These rules are used during drafting and review to prevent modern moral framing,
omniscient narration, or savior dynamics from entering the text.

---

## Chapter 1 — Arrival / Innocence
**POV:** Tommy (teenage, inexperienced)

**High-Risk Bleed**
- Abstract moral language
- Sociological explanations
- Future-oriented judgment

**Flag If Text Includes**
- system, injustice, exploitation, power, rights

**Corrective Action**
- Replace abstraction with sensory detail
- Use confusion or misinterpretation instead of clarity

---

## Chapter 2 — Learning the Ropes
**POV:** Tommy

**High-Risk Bleed**
- Adult authority insight
- Romanticizing hardship

**Flag If Text Includes**
- dignity, resilience (as themes)
- implied moral lessons

**Corrective Action**
- Keep focus on tasks, fatigue, physicality

---

## Chapter 3 — First Fracture
**POV:** Tommy
