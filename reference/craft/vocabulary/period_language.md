## Character-Specific Terms

### Tommy (Musician)
- **Horn**: Trumpet
- **Chops**: Lip strength/skill
- **Blow**: Play (jazz term)
- **Sit in**: Join a band temporarily
- **Charts**: Sheet music
- **Woodshed**: Practice privately

### Jenny (Aerialist)
- **Catcher**: Partner who catches in trapeze act
- **Flyer**: The one who lets go and is caught
- **Bar**: Trapeze bar
- **Trick**: Individual skill/move
- **Mechanic**: Safety harness
- **Web**: Hanging rope for climbing acts
- **Cast**: Return swing to platform

### Rafael (Rigger)
- **Rig**: Set up aerial equipment
- **Strike**: Take down equipment
- **Point**: Direct loading
- **Guywire**: Supporting rope
- **Dead man**: Anchor buried in ground
- **Block and tackle**: Pulley system
