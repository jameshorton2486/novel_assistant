# Motif Tracker
## Recurring Themes and Symbols in "The Price of Silence"

This document tracks recurring motifs, symbols, and thematic elements across the novel.

---

## Core Motifs

### Silence
- **Manifestation:** Tommy's failure to speak, letters that conceal truth
- **Evolution:** From innocence to complicity to burden
- **Resolution:** Breaking silence in 1965 testimony

### Music
- **Manifestation:** Trombone, campfire songs, Rafael's guitar
- **Function:** Connection, communication, cultural bridge
- **Symbolism:** Harmony vs. discord, voice vs. silence

### Artifacts
- **Manifestation:** Postcards, letters, journal fragments
- **Function:** Memory triggers, evidence, testimony
- **Symbolism:** Physical proof of truth, connection to past

### Dignity
- **Manifestation:** Rafael's hidden English, journal, sacrifice
- **Function:** Humanizing exploited workers
- **Symbolism:** Inherent worth regardless of status
